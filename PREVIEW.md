## 🎨 VISUAL EFFECTS IN ACTION

### Color Palette
- **Primary (Cyan)**: `#00D9FF` - Glows, borders, highlights
- **Secondary (Purple)**: `#9D4EDD` - Accents, secondary elements
- **Accent (Yellow)**: `#FFD60A` - Important highlights
- **Success (Green)**: `#06FFA5` - Checkmarks, success states
- **Background**: `#0A0E27` → `#151934` → `#1E2749` (layered)

### Global Effects
- **Scanlines**: Subtle retro CRT effect across entire page
- **Custom Cursor**: Desktop only - outer ring + inner dot with blend mode
- **Scroll Progress**: Gradient bar at top of page
- **Grid Background**: Animated grid on hero section
- **Floating Particles**: Ambient particles throughout
- **Glass Morphism**: Translucent cards with backdrop blur

### Animation Principles
- **NO basic fade-ins** - Each section has unique motion
- **Scroll-driven** - Animations trigger on viewport entry
- **Spring physics** - Natural, bouncy feel for interactions
- **Stagger effects** - Sequential reveals for lists
- **Reduced motion** - Respects user preferences
